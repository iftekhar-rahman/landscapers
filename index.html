Garden design is as much about you, as it is your garden. That's why we start by booking a free of charge initial visit, to discuss your ideas and vision

As part of you FREE initial visit:

  • Discuss your needs, likes and dislikes and assess any potential challenges
  • Photograph the area and keep in your client file
  • Decide how the garden is best surveyed
  • Understand specific requirements such as irrigation, lighting, children’s play areas
  • Discuss how the project will be phased, to suit your budget or seasonal factors
